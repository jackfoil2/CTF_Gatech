In ROP ROP Revolution the user must be able to Make A ROP chain in order to incremet the step counter to 148.
He must make a ROP chain that calls direction function in the ymca.c and call the direction of the step correctly.
Users can find the correct steps to take by either watching the Youtube video in the reference or 
look at the source code itself. After incrementing the steps by 148 the user then must call the win function
which will print out the flag.
